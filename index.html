Does your horse seem grumpy in the saddle? It could be a sign of a poor fit! A well-fitting and comfortable saddle is crucial for both horse and rider; leading to improved performance and a more enjoyable riding experience for everyone.

That's why I take a personalised approach to every consultation, considering your horse's conformation, your riding style, and your specific needs.
